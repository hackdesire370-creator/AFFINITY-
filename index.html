Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, build a vault, upload memories, or communicate with us. This includes photos, videos, and personal letters.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect strictly to provide, maintain, and improve the Affinity Memory Vault service. Your memories are encrypted and securely stored.

3. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration and destruction.

Security

1. End-to-End Encryption

All data stored within your vaults is heavily encrypted. We use industry-standard encryption protocols to ensure that your private memories remain private.

2. Passkey Protection

Your vault is secured by a unique passkey. This passkey is never stored in plain text on our servers. If you lose your passkey, we cannot recover the memories inside the vault.

3. Time-Locked Security

For vaults with a countdown, the server rejects all decryption attempts until the specific server time matches the unlock time, preventing early access even if the passkey is known.
